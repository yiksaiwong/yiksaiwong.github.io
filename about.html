About

I'm Yiksai, a UC Berkeley graduate interested in UI/UX. Creating while solving problems was what sparked my passion for UI/UX design. I focus on creating designs that are clean and simple, yet complex in functionality.

On my spare time, I love reading, watching shows, and editing videos!

My Process

01 Research

Gather information about product and targeted audience. Conduct interviews and perform competitor analysis. Set goals.

02 Analysis

From gathered data, create user personas and storyboard. Outline task flow and user flow.

03 Design

Create wireframe (paper prototype), low-fidelity prototype, then high-fidelity prototype.

04 Test & Reflect

Test product (usability testing), gather feedback, refine project, and reflect.

*Repeat process to improve product.
