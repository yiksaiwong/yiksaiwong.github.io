My Process

01 Research

Gather data and information about the product and define the challenge. Set goals and target audience. Conduct interviews and perform competitor analysis.

02 Analysis

From gathered data, finalize user flow with stakeholders.

03 Design

Create wireframe, low-fidelity prototype, then high-fidelity prototype. Constantly collect feedback throughout the whole design process.

04 Test+Reflect

Test product (usability testing), gather more feedback, refine project, and reflect.

*Repeat this whole process again to update and improve the product. A project is never "finished"!
