Digital Design System

Touch of Modern (TOMO) is an e-commerce website for modern men. Due to the vast amount of new flash sales and frequent promotions on our site, we release daily emails and ads to all platforms (email, app, and web). As the sole designer of this daily task, I created the following system to streamline this process for both myself and other teams.

Role: Digital Designer

01. Style Guides

Because this daily task is a collaboration between various teams (design, photo, copy, merchandising, sales, marketing, and engineering), I had to ensure that all teams were giving me all the necessary information to create the deliverables. Thus, I created the following style guides to help them. Through these guides, teams were able to determine banner sizes, copy character limits, photo layouts, and more.

02. Templates

After all teams have done their parts, a design request is sent to me to create all the necessary creatives. To make the process more efficient, I have created templates to allow me to quickly produce creatives.

Promotional and Email Banner Template
Digital Ads Template

03. Feedback

After creating a few drafts, there are multiple rounds of feedback between myself, marketing, and merchandising. At this step, we discuss and finalize which design and layout works best.

Promotional Banner Drafts

04. Final Checks & Deliverables

After the rounds of feedback, one final check is done by multiple people to ensure that the copy and banner sizes are correct before exporting the assets. After the files are exported, the final step is to check that all banners are being displayed correctly in all platforms. If there are no errors, then woo hoo, we are done!
